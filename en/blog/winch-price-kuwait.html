One of the most common questions we receive daily is: how much does towing cost in Kuwait? It's a fair question — when your car breaks down, you want to know the cost before you call. In this guide we explain transparently how flatbed and towing prices are determined in Kuwait, the factors that affect them, and how to get the best service at a fair price.

What Factors Determine the Flatbed Price?

The towing price is not a fixed number — it depends on several logical factors:

1. Distance

The greater the distance between your car's location and the destination, the slightly higher the price. Recovery within the same area is the cheapest, while transport from one distant governorate to another is higher due to road time and fuel.

2. Type of Car

Regular and family cars have a standard price. Lowered sports cars and luxury vehicles require a full-down flatbed that lowers completely to ground level to protect them, and these may have a special rate due to the extra care. We are also equipped to transport electric vehicles up to the latest models.

3. Car Condition

A parked but intact car differs from one that is fully disabled and won't move at all, or a car damaged after an accident that needs special loading equipment. The harder the loading, the price may be slightly affected.

4. Time

Our service is available 24/7, but emergency services late at night or in difficult conditions may be slightly higher than regular daytime service.
