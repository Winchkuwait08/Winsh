Transporting Sports & Luxury Cars with a Full-Down Flatbed

Last updated: June 2026 · 3 min read

Sports, luxury and lowered cars are not like ordinary cars — they require special care during transport. A simple loading mistake can cause scratches or costly damage to the front bumper or chassis. That's why the full-down flatbed exists. In this article we explain why it's the right choice for your valuable car.

What Is a Full-Down Flatbed?

A full-down flatbed is a modern hydraulic flatbed that can lower completely until it touches the ground, creating a very low loading angle. This means the car drives onto the bed smoothly without its underside scraping the ground — crucial for lowered cars.

Why Do Luxury Cars Need a Full-Down Flatbed?

  • Protects the front bumper: sports cars sit very low, and a regular flatbed may scratch the front bumper. Full-down solves this completely.
  • Preserves the chassis: the low loading angle prevents any pressure or friction on the car's body.
  • Safe wheel transport: the car is secured with dedicated straps that protect expensive rims and tyres.
  • Suitable for electric vehicles: we transport EVs up to the latest models following the manufacturer's instructions.
