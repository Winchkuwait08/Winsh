Tyre Change Service Kuwait — Across All of Kuwait

A flat tyre doesn't have to ruin your day or leave you struggling on the roadside. We come to your location, change the tyre to your spare or repair it on the spot, and get you safely back on the road in minutes.

Change to your spare

If you have a spare, we fit it properly with the right tools, torque the wheel correctly and check it's safe before you drive on. No kneeling on a hot roadside or wrestling with a stuck nut.

On-the-spot repair

If the puncture can be repaired and you have no usable spare, we can often fix it where you are, so a small nail doesn't turn into a long wait or a tow.

Safe on fast roads

A blowout on a highway is dangerous to deal with alone. We reach you quickly, work safely at the roadside, and get you moving again without you having to risk the traffic.

And a tow if needed

If the tyre and wheel are too damaged to fix in place, we can tow the car to a tyre shop instead. One call to 60601740 covers either outcome, day or night.
