Broken Car Recovery Kuwait — Across All of Kuwait

A car that simply won't go — engine trouble, a seized gearbox, an electrical fault — needs proper recovery, not a push. We tow fully disabled cars safely onto a flatbed and move them to your workshop or destination, with no strain on the vehicle.

When the car won't move at all

Some breakdowns leave a car completely stuck, with wheels that won't turn or a gearbox that won't release. A flatbed is the right answer here, because it lifts the whole car off the ground and carries it without forcing anything.

Safe for the gearbox and wheels

Towing a disabled car the wrong way can cause real damage, especially to automatic gearboxes. Our flatbed avoids all of that by carrying the car rather than dragging it, protecting the parts that are most at risk.

To your workshop of choice

You decide where the car goes — your usual workshop, a dealership, or home. We load it carefully, secure it properly and deliver it there, anywhere in Kuwait.

Around the clock

Breakdowns happen at any time, so we're available 24 hours. Call 60601740 or message on WhatsApp, describe the problem and your location, and we'll send the nearest flatbed to recover your car.
