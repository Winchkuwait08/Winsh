Towing Service Prices Kuwait — Across All of Kuwait

Worrying about the bill shouldn't add to the stress of a breakdown. We keep our pricing simple and transparent: you're told the final price up front, it's based on clear factors, and nothing is added afterward. Here's how it works.

Priced before we start

We tell you the final price before the service begins, not after it's done. You agree it first, so there's never a shock at the end and never a fee you didn't expect.

What the price depends on

Two things mainly shape the price: the distance involved and the type of vehicle. A short move across town and a transport between governorates aren't the same, and a small car and a heavy 4x4 aren't either — so we price each job fairly for what it actually involves.

No hidden fees

What we quote is what you pay. There are no vague extras added at the end and no surprise charges. Transparency is the basis of trust, and we'd rather you call us again than feel caught out.

Get a price now

The simplest way to know the cost is to ask. Call 60601740 or message on WhatsApp with your location, destination and car type, and we'll give you a clear price straight away — any time, day or night.
